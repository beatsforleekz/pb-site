Carl Ryden

LA-based and originally from Sweden, Carl Ryden is a BMI Award-winning, Grammy-nominated, multi-platinum-selling and chart-topping producer, songwriter and musician who has established himself as one of the industry’s most respected talents. Working across both sides of the Atlantic, Carl has collaborated with world-renowned artists across pop, dance and electronic music.

His credits include Kylie Minogue’s “Just Imagine” from her UK and Australian No.1 Gold-certified album, Solardo’s multi-million-streaming club anthem “Power”, Ricky Martin and Maluma’s Platinum-certified and Grammy-nominated “Vente Pa Ca”, Sigma’s UK Gold-certified album, and Steps’ UK No.1 “Scared Of The Dark” from their Gold-certified album, among many others.

To date, Carl has contributed to over 10 billion streams worldwide, alongside multiple Gold, Platinum and Diamond-certified records, chart-performing releases, award-winning and nominated projects, and numerous global sync placements.

Known for his work ethic and professionalism, Carl is highly regarded among artists, labels and managers worldwide. Consistent in both creativity and delivery, he continues to surpass expectations across every project he works on.

Robin M

Recognised talent Robin Michelangelo aka Robin M is a London-based DJ and producer known for his distinctive fusion of deep, percussive and melodic house. Drawing inspiration from his British, Italian and Ethiopian heritage, and shaped by London’s vibrant music scene, Robin’s sound is authentic and forward-thinking.

Founder of ULU Records, a fast-rising underground label and community, Robin has guided the label to chart-topping releases on Traxsource and Beatport, earning platinum and gold certifications. In 2024, he launched the ULU Rising event series, delivering intimate and full capacity sized sold-out events.

Robin has also collaborated with Bedouin and released music on their respected label Human By Default as well as Diplo’s Higher Ground. His tracks are played in top clubs and festivals, supported by BBC Radio 1 and tastemaker DJs including Black Coffee, Bedouin, Dennis Cruz and many more.

As a DJ, Robin is known for delivering immersive journeys and a strong understanding of the floor, blending his own productions with carefully curated records. He has performed at places ranging from Bedouin’s SAGA event, Pacha Ibiza, Blue Marlin, Club Chinois, Nyege Nyege Festival, and headlined shows across Kenya, South Africa and the UK, including the legendary Fabric.

Robin has shared stages with luminaries such as Bedouin, Shimza, Pete Tong, Reznik (Keinemusik), Nandu, Notre Dame, Tripolism and Caiiro. Driven by a passion for music and connection, he creates moments that resonate long after the last track fades.
